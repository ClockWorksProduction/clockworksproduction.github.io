About Us

ClockWorksProduction (CWP) is a creative digital collective blending entertainment and education through immersive storytelling. Founded in 2021, what began as a passion project has grown into a multi-branch studio specializing in games, animation, and interactive media. With a focus on innovation, narrative, and atmospheric design, CWP is dedicated to crafting meaningful and engaging experiences across every medium we touch.

Our team is made up of storytellers, coders, artists, and dreamers — all working together to bring compelling, eerie, and unforgettable experiences to life. Whether you're delving into one of our command-line horror games, watching animated tales unfold, or following our VTuber talent, we’re here to blur the line between fiction and reality.
