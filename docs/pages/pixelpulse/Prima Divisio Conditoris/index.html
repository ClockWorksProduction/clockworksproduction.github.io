Foundational Archive

The Prima Divisio Conditoris, known also as the First Founders Division, is the earliest and most essential cadre of the Clockwork Production VTuber Collective. The division represents the core architects of creativity — the origin of ideas, the designers of worlds, and the eternal keepers of the studio’s legacy.

Chapter Lore

In the early cycles of the Clockwork Continuum, when the digital æther was formless, it was the Prima Divisio Conditoris who forged the first threads. They shaped concepts, crafted characters, and engineered narratives—establishing the framework from which all other divisions would eventually emerge.

To this day, members of the Prima Divisio Conditoris are seen as architects and engineers of creativity, combining precision, imagination, and a deep respect for the foundational mechanisms that power the Collective.

Chapter Heraldic Achievement & Symbolism

The insignia of the Prima Divisio Conditoris is a stylized golden cog encircling a central spark — symbolizing the union of engineering and inspiration.

  • Golden Cog — the eternal mechanism
  • Central Spark — the birth of creation
  • Outer Ring — the continuity of legacy

The cog is always depicted in PDC Gold (#d3b56d), representing nobility and craftsmanship.
